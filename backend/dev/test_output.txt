**Core (required) courses for the Biology major**

| Course # | Title (abbreviated) | Units |
|---------|----------------------|-------|
| **BIOL 2960** | Principles of Biology I | **4** |
| **BIOL 2970** | Principles of Biology II | **4** |
| **CHEM 1601** *or* **CHEM 1701** | General Chemistry I (Principles of General Chemistry I) | **3** |
| **CHEM 1602** *or* **CHEM 1702** | General Chemistry II (Principles of General Chemistry II) | **3** |
| **CHEM 1751** | General Chemistry Laboratory I | **2** |
| **CHEM 1752** | General Chemistry Laboratory II | **2** |
| **CHEM 2561** | Organic Chemistry I With Lab | **4** |
| **CHEM 2562** | Organic Chemistry II With Lab | **4** |
| **PHYSICS 1740** *or* **PHYSICS 1760** | Physics I (or Focused Physics I) | **3** |
| **PHYSICS 1741** | Physics I Lab | **1** |
| **PHYSICS 1742** *or* **PHYSICS 1762** | Physics II (or Focused Physics II) | **3** |
| **PHYSICS 1743** | Physics II Lab | **1** |
| **MATH 1510** | Calculus II | **3** |
| **MATH 2130** *or* **MATH 3020** *or* **SDS 2020** | Calculus III / Elementary‑to‑Intermediate Statistics / Elementary Probability & Statistics | **3** |
| **Total core‑course units** | | **40** |

These 40 units of coursework are the **core requirements** that every Biology major must complete.  

In addition to the core, the major requires:

* **At least 18 units** of advanced biology courses (numbered 3000 or above).  
* **One advanced laboratory course** from the approved list.  

The advanced‑biology and advanced‑lab units together bring the total major requirement to **58 units**.
