The complete degree requirements for the Biology Major with Specialization in Genomics and Computational Biology, B.A. are as follows:

**Total Units Required:** 58

**Grade Requirement:** All courses counted toward the major must be taken for a letter grade if offered, and a grade of C- or better must be earned in all of these courses.

**Required Courses:**

*   BIOL 2960: Principles of Biology I (4 units)
*   BIOL 2970: Principles of Biology II (4 units)
*   CHEM 1601: Principles of General Chemistry I OR CHEM 1701: General Chemistry I (3 units)
*   CHEM 1602: Principles of General Chemistry II OR CHEM 1702: General Chemistry II (3 units)
*   CHEM 1751: General Chemistry Laboratory I (2 units)
*   CHEM 1752: General Chemistry Laboratory II (2 units)
*   CHEM 2561: Organic Chemistry I With Lab (4 units)
*   CHEM 2562: Organic Chemistry II With Lab (4 units)
*   PHYSICS 1740: Physics I OR PHYSICS 1760: Focused Physics I (3 units)
*   PHYSICS 1741: Physics I Lab (1 unit)
*   PHYSICS 1742: Physics II OR PHYSICS 1762: Focused Physics II (3 units)
*   PHYSICS 1743: Physics II Lab (1 unit)
*   MATH 1510: Calculus II (3 units)
*   MATH 2130: Calculus III OR MATH 3020: Elementary to Intermediate Statistics and Data Analysis OR SDS 2020: Elementary Probability and Statistics (3 units)

**Total Units for Required Courses:** 40 units

**Advanced Biology Courses:** At least 18 units in advanced biology courses (numbered 3000 or above) are required. These units may not include BIOL 3030, BIOL 3430, BIOL 3990, BIOL 3991, BIOL 3900, BIOL 4990, or BIOL 4991; courses in the School of Continuing &amp; Professional Studies; or more than 3 units of history-of-science courses. The following courses originating in other departments count towards these 18 units: PHYSICS 3354, PHYSICS 3324, ANTHRO 4581, CHEM 4810, CHEM 4820, and CHEM 4833. Up to 6 units of independent research (BIOL 4950, BIOL 4951, BIOL 4952, BIOL 4953, or BIOL 4954) may be counted, and up to 3 units of BIOL 49XX seminar courses may be counted.

**Specialization Requirements (Genomics and Computational Biology):**

*   **Total units required (including biology major requirements):** 64
*   **Additional requirements:**
    *   An advanced genomics/computational biology elective: BIOL 3240, BIOL 4183, BIOL 4344, BIOL 5480, or BIOL 5488.
    *   Statistics: SDS 2200 or SDS 3200.
    *   Two outside electives: CSE 1301 and CSE 2407.
    *   CSE 2400 is strongly recommended.
    *   The advanced laboratory course used to fulfill the major requirement must be one of the following: BIOL 3492, BIOL 3493, BIOL 4220, BIOL 4342, BIOL 4343, BIOL 4346, BIOL 4522, or BIOL 4525.
